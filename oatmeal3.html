Cucumber Witch Hazel Toner, perfect for soothing, hydrating, and refreshing your skin. Here's how you can create your own Cucumber Witch Hazel Toner at home.

How to Make a Cucumber Witch Hazel Toner

  • Step 1:Peel and blend 1/2 a cucumber into a smooth puree. Strain it through a fine mesh or cheesecloth to extract the juice.
  • Step 2:Combine 1/4 cup of cucumber juice with 1/4 cup of alcohol-free witch hazel in a bowl.
  • Step 3: (Optional) Add 1 tablespoon of rose water for extra hydration and a pleasant scent.
  • Step 4:(Optional) Add 2-3 drops of essential oil like lavender (for soothing) or tea tree (for acne-prone skin).
  • Step 5:Mix the ingredients well and pour into a sterilized spray bottle or glass container.
  • Step 6: Store in the refrigerator for up to 7 days for maximum freshness. Shake before use.
  • Step 7:Apply after cleansing your face by spritzing it directly or using a cotton pad to gently swipe across your skin
