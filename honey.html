This mask is ideal for moisturizing dry skin, reducing redness, and giving a natural glow! Would. Here's how you can create your own honey face mask at home.

How to Make a Honey Face Mask

  • Step 1:Take 2 tablespoons of raw, organic honey and place it in a small bowl..
  • Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice for brightening properties (optional for sensitive skin).
  • Step 3:Mix the ingredients thoroughly until you have a smooth consistency.
  • Step 4:Apply the mixture evenly to your face using your fingers or a brush, avoiding the eye area.
  • Step 5:Leave the mask on for 15-20 minutes.
  • Step 6: Rinse your face with lukewarm water, gently massaging in circular motions. Pat dry with a clean towel.
