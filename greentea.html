Greentea face mask fights free radicals and reduces signs of aging. Here's how you can create your own greentea face mask at home.

How to Make a Greentea Face Mask

  • Step 1: Brew 1 green tea bag in 1/4 cup of hot water and let it cool completely.
  • Step 2:In a small bowl, mix 1 tablespoon of the brewed green tea with 1 tablespoon of honey.
  • Step 3:Add 1 teaspoon of rice flour or oatmeal for gentle exfoliation and to thicken the mask.
  • Step 4: Stir the mixture thoroughly until you achieve a smooth paste.
  • Step 5:Apply the mask evenly to your face using a brush or your fingers, avoiding the eye area.
  • Step 6:Let the mask sit for 15-20 minutes or until it starts to dry slightly.
  • Step 7:Rinse off with lukewarm water, gently massaging in circular motions for light exfoliation. Pat your face dry with a clean towel.
