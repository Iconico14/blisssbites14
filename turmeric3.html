How to Make a Witch Hazel Toner

  • Step 1:In a clean bowl, combine 1/4 cup of alcohol-free witch hazel (to avoid drying out the skin).
  • Step 2: Add 2 tablespoons of rose water or distilled water for added hydration.
  • Step 3: (Optional) Add 2-3 drops of essential oil, like lavender (soothing) or tea tree (anti-acne), for a customized touch.
  • Step 4:Mix well and pour into a sterilized glass bottle or spray bottle for easy application.
  • Step 5: Use it as a toner after cleansing your face. Apply with a cotton pad or spritz directly onto your skin.
