Brown sugar gently removes dead skin cells, making it suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin. Here's how you can create your own Brown Sugar Scrub at home.

How to Make a Brown Sugar Scrub

  • Step 1: Combine 2 tablespoons of brown sugar with 1 tablespoon of coconut oil or olive oil in a bowl.
  • Step 2:Add 1 teaspoon of honey for extra hydration and antibacterial benefits.
  • Step 3:(Optional) Add a few drops of vanilla extract or essential oil (e.g., lavender, orange) for a soothing or refreshing scent.
  • Step 4:Mix the ingredients thoroughly until they form a grainy paste.
  • Step 5: Apply the scrub to damp skin using circular motions, focusing on dry or rough areas like elbows, knees, and heels.
  • Step 6: Rinse off with warm water and pat your skin dry. Follow up with a moisturizer for extra softness.
