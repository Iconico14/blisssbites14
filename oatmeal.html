Oatmeal calms irritation and redness, making it ideal for sensitive skin. Here's how you can create your own oatmeal face mask at home.

How to Make a oatmeal Face Mask

  • Step 1: Grind 2 tablespoons of oatmeal into a fine powder (or use instant oatmeal).
  • Step 2:Mix the oatmeal powder with 1 tablespoon of honey and 1-2 tablespoons of milk or yogurt to create a thick paste.
  • Step 3:(Optional) Add 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric or a few drops of almond oil for extra nourishment.
  • Step 4: Apply the paste evenly to your face using your fingers or a brush, avoiding the eye area.
  • Step 5:Let the mask sit for 15-20 minutes.
  • Step 5:Rinse off with lukewarm water, gently massaging your skin in circular motions to exfoliate. Pat dry with a clean towel.
