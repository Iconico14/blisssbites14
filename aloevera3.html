Aloe Vera Toner, perfect for hydrating and soothing the skin while restoring its natural balance Here's how you can create your own Aloe Vera Toner at home.

How to Make a Aloe Vera Toner

  • Step 1:Mix 1/4 cup of pure aloe vera gel (fresh or store-bought, organic) with 1/4 cup of distilled water or rose water in a bowl.
  • Step 2:(Optional) Add 2-3 drops of essential oil like lavender (calming), tea tree (anti-acne), or chamomile (soothing).
  • Step 3:Stir the mixture until fully blended and pour it into a sterilized glass bottle or spray bottle.
  • Step 4:Store the toner in the refrigerator for a refreshing cooling effect during application.
  • Step 5:After cleansing your face, apply the toner with a cotton pad or spray it directly onto your skin. Let it air dry or gently pat it in.
